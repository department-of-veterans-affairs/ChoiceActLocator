Eligibility

Some benefits may also be used by active Servicemembers, Reservists, and National Guard members as well as eligible spouses, children, and survivors of Veterans and Servicemembers. Consult the VA benefits booklet or visit the nearest VA regional office for character of discharge requirements and more detailed information.
